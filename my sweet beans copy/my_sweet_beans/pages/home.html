MY SWEET BEANS 

Let's talk about life. Bean at it for decades.

Mrs. Kecia Baldwin

Podcast creator and Producer

Dreams have meaning. For years I dreamed about planting beans, and never understood why. I looked up the meaning and it meant you are putting in the work now, but will reap the rewards later. In life we expect immediate gratification, but sometimes we must be patient for sweeter things to come.
